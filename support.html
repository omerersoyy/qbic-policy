Frequently asked questions

How do I get past a level?
Finish the tunnel before the timer runs out. Swipe left/right/up to switch walls and swipe down to dodge through demons. Time is the only thing you need to beat — destroying every demon is optional.
What are the fish for?
Fish are coins you earn by dodging demons. Spend them on the level Preview to scout a level before you run it.
How does the Preview work?
Tap the eye button to preview the upcoming level. If you're out of fish, you can watch a short ad to earn more.
My progress looks wrong / I lost progress.
Progress is saved on your device. If something looks off, email us with your device model and what you saw — it really helps us fix it fast.
How do I turn the music or sound off?
Open Settings from the home screen (the gear icon) to toggle music and sound effects.
